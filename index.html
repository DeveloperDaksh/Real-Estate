About Us

Reasons to choose

Legacy Luxury Homes has been designing and building happy homes since 1987. We offer premium quality builds that lasts a lifetime and leads to a peaceful and hassle-free experience for homeowners so that you can focus on important things, that is creating everlasting happy memories in your new house.

We manifest our passion of homebuilding in everything we do. Each and everyone in our team, from the president to the architects, from the designers to the sales staff, and all the tradespeople live by the same philosophy. the intention of exceeding customer expectations.

Our team works tirelessly to ensure that every home and every community is built to the highest standards we have set for ourselves, and continue to maintain, since 1987.

We are fully invested in staying modern and work continuously to incorporate the latest build technologies in our homes. We are member of all important house building association in the province of Ontario and Canada.

We intend to bring all our experience and hard work in your service to offer you the best quality homes that you will cherish for years to come
